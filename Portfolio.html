Biography

While I may like the sciences, I have always had a side interest in computers and wanted to learn about how to build them, coding and other cool things about them. I have an older brother, younger sister and brother along with living with my mom and dad. Even though my parents came to America as immigrants, I hope to make the best out of every opportunity and resource that I can get so I can become a successful and inspiring practitioner. My hobbies are playing basketball with friends, watching day in the life vlogs of aspiring doctors, and just having a good time with people.
